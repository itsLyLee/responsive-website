Desserts

This is a blog for the sweettoothed munchiekins lovers, where you never can get enough of cookies, cakes, or desserts! My love for sweets has always been there since a very young age. If you were looking for me, you would definitely find me by the dessert table as a kid munching on every cake there is! As I grew older, unfortunately, I suffered a very severe skin condition called Topical Stereoid Withdrawal along with Atopic Eczema, which left me still at home and sick in bed for over a year, and still recovering. This saddened me somewhat extremely, also because it prevented me from enjoying all the food and sweets I have loved since a child. Almost a whole depressed year went by, and long story short, I felt I had enough of the dull days, woke up and said "I have cravings for sweets!!!!", decided to explore other recipes which contained no gluten, dairy or sugar, and this of course led me to creating Sweetside! Not only was this website created for keeping myself busy during my dull days, but I also wanted a place to store my favourite recipes, haha! I must also mention all the recipes are collected from my very favourite the Minimalist Baker! Can we all bow-down to all these simple amazing recipes!? Not only that, they have so many great gluten, dairy and sugar free recipes for those that are sensitive like me - yey! So you are very welcome sweetypie! (you see what I did there?) Now, enjoy the Sweetside munchiekin.
