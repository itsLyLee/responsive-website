Ly Loves

Desserts

This is a blog for the sweettoothed lovers, where cookies, cakes, or desserts brings you love and joy! My love for cakes and desserts has always been there since a very young age. If you were looking for me, you would definitely find me by the dessert table as a kid munching on every cake there is! As I grew older, unfortunately, I suffered a very severe skin condition called Topical Stereoid Withdrawal along with Atopic Eczema, which left me still at home and sick in bed for over a year. This saddened me somewhat extremely, also because it prevented me from enjoying all the food and sweets I have loved since a child. Almost a whole depressed year went by, and long story short, I felt I had enough of the dull days, woke up and said "I have cravings for sweets!!!!". This of course led me to Sweetside! So, in order to brighten my dull sick days, I decided to explore other recipes which contained no gluten, dairy or sugar, and before I knew it I collected a few recipes and then came along to create this website in order to keep myself busy, haha! Now you know my story, and for the rest of it - that is history. So you are very welcome sweetypie! (you see what I did there?)
